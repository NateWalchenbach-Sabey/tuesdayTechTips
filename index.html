Tip: A message can look polished and still be suspicious. Focus on the request, the sender, and whether the message fits the situation.

Sabey-safe habits

  • Pause on urgent requests that pressure you to act immediately.
  • Check sender names, domains, and unexpected attachments.
  • Never share passwords or MFA codes through email or chat.
  • Verify financial or HR changes using a trusted method you already know.
  • Report suspicious messages using your normal internal process.
